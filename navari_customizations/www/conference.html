About Us

Frappe is a remote technology company committed to building world-class open-source software products and services. It is the company behind ERPNext, the world’s best free and open source ERP.

Navari is the first ERPNext Frappe Partner in Kenya, and has more than 25 clients running on ERPNext. As a Frappe Certifield Partner, Navari provides ERPNext implementation and customization services, business process analysis and mapping, systems integration, technical delivery experience and innovation to deliver value and innovation on ERPNext, with a focus on ERP Solutions for Small and mid-sized enterprises.

Erpchampions is Uganda's premier Frappe Certified Partner. They expertly implement ERPNext solutions for businesses.
